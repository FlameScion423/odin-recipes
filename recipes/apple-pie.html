Apple pie

Home

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 package pastry for a double crust (9-inch) pie
  • 5 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. combine white sugar, brown sugar, butter, water, cinnamon, and salt in a sucepan over medium heat
  3. bring to a boil, remove from heat, and set aside
  4. line the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie plate with one crust
  5. fill with apple slices
  6. roll out top crust into a 10-inch circle. cut into 8 1-inch wide strips
  7. weave the pastry one at a time into a lattice and fold theends under the edge of the bottom crustand crimp to seal
  8. pour butter sugar mixture over the lattice, letting it drizzle into the filling
  9. bake for 15 minutes. reduce heat to 350 degrees F and continue baking until crust is golden brown(35 to 40 minutes)
