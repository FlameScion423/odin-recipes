Pancakes

Home

ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten

directions

  1. combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
  2. make a well in the center, pour in milk, oil, and egg and mix until smooth
  3. heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat
  4. pour a scoop(about 1/4 cup) per pancake and cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until browned
  5. serve and enjoy
