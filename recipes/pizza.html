Description

easy recipe for pizza dough, sauce, and toppings

Pizza dough ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 packet(2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups bread flour
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Pizza dough directions

  1. Add warm water to bowl with yeast and rest for 5 minutes to bloom the yeast
  2. Add flour, salt, sugar, and olive oil to the bowl and knead well
  3. Place dough in an oiled bowl and let rise in a warm place for 1 1/2 hours

Pizza sauce ingredients

  • 1 can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • t tsp dried oregano
  • pinch red pepper flakes
  • salt
  • 1 onion, chopped
